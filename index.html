Bio

Sheikh Rabiul Islam is a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at the Tennessee Tech University . His Ph.D. advisers are Dr. William (Bill) Eberle and Dr. Sheikh Khaled Ghafoor. He is associated with the Knowledge Discovery Lab. His research focuses on Data Mining and Big Data analysis. Particular topics include: predictive modeling in finance and cyber security, hierarchical and longitudinal data analysis, anomaly or fraud detection, and text mining.

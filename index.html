Bio

Sheikh Rabiul Islam is a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at the Tennessee Tech University . His Ph.D. advisers are Dr. William (Bill) Eberle and Dr. Sheikh Khaled Ghafoor. He is associated with the Knowledge Discovery Lab. His research focuses on Big Data and Data Mining. Particular topics include: interpretable and explainable Artificial Intelligence, predictive modeling in finance and cyber security, anomaly or fraud detection.
